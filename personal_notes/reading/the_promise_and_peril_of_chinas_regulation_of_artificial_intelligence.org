#+TITLE: The Promise And Peril of China's Regulation of Artificial Intelligence

* Introduction

- China has emerged as a pioneer in AI regulation
- Unveiled the first regulation specifically for recommendation systems in 2021
- The Chinese Cyberspace Administration (CAC) introduced laws addressing the production of deepfakes in 2023
- When ChatGPT launched in November 2022, this prompted another flurry of legislation and rulemaking
- Within 3 months, the Chinese government had a comprehensive framework for regulating generative AI
  - Security assessments
  - Algorithms need to be registered with the CAC
- China is the only country in the world that requires ex-ante licensing for AIs
