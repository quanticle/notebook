#+TITLE: 2023 Formula 1 Drivers' Championship Predictions

** Likely Winners
1. Max Verstappen --- Red Bull
   - Unlikely that Red Bull will go backwards relative to other teams in the absence of major aero or engine rule changes
   - Even if Mercedes catch up this year, Max showed in 2021 that he could go toe-to-toe with Lewis and win in equivalent machinery
2. Lewis Hamilton --- Mercedes
   - Mercedes will probably be better this year compared with 2022
   - The team still has excellent discipline and good decision-making; it's just that their car sucked
   - Lewis wants the ocho
     - So he can be the GOAT
     - So he can lay the ghost of Abu Dhabi 2021 to rest
   - Has a much better team-mate in George Russell than he did in Valteri Bottas, but that might actually be a disavantage (see below)
3. George Russell --- Mercedes
   - Somewhat unlikely, given that Lewis is still the #1 driver at Mercedes
   - Still, George is skilled and ambitious; so he might go for it if the car is fast
   - Will be interesting to see how he and Hamilton get along if the car is genuinely good and a championship is on the line --- Hamilton vs. Rosberg 2.0, or has Lewis mellowed with age?
4. Charles LeClerc --- Ferrari
   - 2022 was a golden opportunity for LeClerc and he and Ferrari let it slip
   - Ferrari had an engine advantage at the beginning of the season that they squandered through a combination of LeClerc's mistakes (France) and the team's poor race-day decision-making (Monaco, Great Britain)
   - Unlikely to have a similar advantage in 2023
   - Firing Binotto was probably the wrong move --- Ferrari actually achieved their objective for the season (being a contender for the World Championship), but the team was still punished for not achieving more
   - LeClerc has the talent, but he needs to stop making stupid mistakes
   - Ferrari has the talent, but the corporate side needs to stop treating team principals like football managers
   - It says a lot about how badly I think Ferrari has screwed this up that I rank /both/ the Mercedes drivers higher, even though the 2022 Merc was an absolute travesty of a car
