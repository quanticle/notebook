* Water Heater

** Pilot Light Reignition
- Turn switch clockwise to the off position (make sure to press firmly to get around the detent)
- Turn switch counterclockwise to "PILOT"
- Press down the pilot light button and hit the the igniter switch
- Look through the watch glass to see if the pilot light has ignited

** Thermocouple Failure
- If the pilot light keeps going out, the problem could be a thermocouple failure
- The thermocouple is a device that coverts heat to voltage; it's used to detect if the pilot light is on
- If the gas valve does not detect that the pilot light is on, it will shut down the water heater to ensure that unburnt gas does not accumulate in the building
