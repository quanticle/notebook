#+TITLE: Smoke Detectors

- To minimize false alarms look for a smoke detector with a photoelectric sensor
- Look for something that complies with the UL 217 10th edition standard, as that standard includes testing for "nuisance alarms"
  - Look for "reduced nuisance alarms" if the detector doesn't specifically mention UL 217 10th edition
- Clean the detector periodically with a compressed gas duster
