#+TITLE: Cleaning Tips

* Dishwasher Cleaning

** Vinegar & Baking Soda
- Empty dishwasher
- Put 1 cup (230ml) of vinegar into a dishwasher-safe bowl and put it in the top rack of the dishwasher
- Run the hottest cycle, but skip the drying step
- Open the dishwasher door and let it air dry
- Then, sprinkle 1 cup of baking soda on the floor of the dishwasher
- Run the hottest cycle again, and once again skip the drying step
- Open the dishwasher door and let it air dry

* Floor Tile Grout
- Use Chlorox bleach toilet bowl cleaner
- Squeeze some onto the grout between tiles
- Scrub with a toothbrush
- Another grout-cleaning solution is 1 part 99.5% anhydrous isopropyl alcohol mixed with 1 part Dawn dish detergent mixed with two parts water
- Some sources say you should /not/ use toilet bowl cleaner on grout because it is caustic and can damage the grout over time
- There are also commercial tile and grout cleaners
