* Office Chair Gas Cylinder Replacement
- Spray WD-40 into the joint between the chair and the cylinder
- Use a pipe wrench to turn the cylinder 1/4 to 1/2 turn
- The cylinder should just pop out of the chair at that point
- Use a mallet to remove the old cylinder from the base
- To install the new cylinder, place the new cylinder into the base, and then put the chair on top of the cylinder
