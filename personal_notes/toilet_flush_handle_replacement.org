#+TITLE: Toilet Flush Handle Replacement

- When buying a flush handle, ensure that it has
  - Flush handle
  - Tank lever
  - Tank lever nut
- Adjustable wrench
- Remove tank lid
- Disconnect flapper chain from old tank lever and hook it over the toilet's overflow tube
- Unscrew the tank lever nut with the adjustable wrench (lever nut is reverse-threaded)
- Remove the nut from the flush handle and remove the handle and tank lever from the toilet
- Install the new toilet handle by inserting the new tank lever into the tank and align the bolt to correctly fit on the tank
- Slide the new lever nut over the tank lever and screw it onto the shank of the bolt
- Tighten the nut a little over hand tight with the adjustable wrench
- Reattach flapper chain
- Test the toilet flush
