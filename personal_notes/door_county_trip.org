#+TITLE: Door County Trip

* Trip Dates
- 2024-09-27 --- 2024-09-29
- Effectively we'll be there for 2024-09-28, as 09-27 and 09-29 will be travel

* Location
- Harbor Inn, Algoma
- Approximately 6 hours from home

* Things To Do
- Scenic drives
  - State highway 42 (west side)
  - State highway 57 (east side)
- Apple picking
  - There are a lot of apple orchards in Door County
  - Mostly concentrated along Highway 42
- Lighthouses
  - Cana Island lighthouse
  - Eagle Bluff lighthouse
- Visit cute towns
  - Fish creek
  - Ephraim
  - Bailey's Harbor
  - Sturgeon Bay
- Ferry to Washington Island

* Detailed Plan
- Friday 2024-09-27
  - Leave home around 3pm
  - Eat dinner around Wausau, WI (+3 hours), or maybe even Green Bay (+4.5 hours)
  - Arrive at Harbor Inn around 9pm
- Saturday 2024-09-28
  - Drive to Washington Island
    - 1hr 25 minute drive from hotel to ferry terminal
    - Ferries to Washington Island leave on the hour, 8:00 am - 5:00pm
    - Schoolhouse Beach --- distinct white stones
    - Rock Island
      - Ferries at 10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm
      - Ferries returning at 10:15am 12:15pm, 2:15pm, 4:15pm
  - On the way back
    - Stop at Peninsula State Park and climb the Eagle Tower canopy walk
    - Visit the Cana Island Lighthouse, open from 10 am to 5pm
      - If we can make it by 3pm, we can ride a tractor across the causeway
      - Else if the causeway is dry we can walk across
    - Or just drive around --- the road is supposed to be very scenic
