* Shower Cartridge Replacement

- My shower cartridge: =Delta RP-19804=

** Directions
1. Turn off water at main
   - Turn off mains water
   - Turn off output from hot water heater
2. Drain pipes by running, in order
   1. Kitchen faucet
   2. Lower bathroom faucet
   3. Upstairs bathroom faucet
   4. Shower
3. Take faceplate off shower knob
4. Remove screw
5. Remove shower knob
6. Remove trim sleeve with strap wrench
7. Use strap wrench to loosen bonnet nut holding the shower cartridge in place
8. Grab valve cartridge firmly and wiggle it to get it to come loose (/this might take a lot of pulling and fiddling/)
   - /Helps to use the strap wrench to gain additional grip/
9. Install the new cartridge, making sure the hot side is to the *left*
10. Adjust the spacer on the new cartridge to set the maximum temperature of the shower
11. Reinstall bonnet nut and tighten by hand fully
12. Reinstall trim sleeve
13. Reinstall shower knob
14. Reattach shower knob screw
15. Reinstall shower knob faceplate
16. Turn water back on

** Further shower noise
- Shower making same noise as prior to cartridge replacement
- Lower pitch and volume
- Turning on the faucet makes the noise go away
- Noise comes back instantly when faucet is shut
- Shower also emits a high pitched hissing noise
