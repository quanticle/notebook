#+TITLE: How to dice a tomato

* Technique 1
- Cut the tomato into rings
- Slice up each ring

* Technique 2
- Slice the top off the tomato
- Slice the tomato, stopping approximately 1/4 from the bottom
- Rotate 90°, then repeat
- Put the tomato on its side then slice downwards
- Slice with a sawing motion
- Slice up the end separately
- /This seems like it requires a really sharp knife/
- /It seems like the key is to minimize downwards pressure and slice using a sawing motion back and forth/
