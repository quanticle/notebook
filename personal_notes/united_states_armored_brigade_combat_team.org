#+TITLE: United States Armored Brigade Combat Team Organization

* 1st Armored Brigade Combat Team
- 6th Squadron, 1st Cavalry Regiment --- Cavalry squadron
- 2nd Battalion, 37th Armor Regiment --- Armor battalion
- 4th Battalion, 70th Armor Regiment --- Armor battalion
- 1st Battalion, 36th Infantry Regiment --- Mechanized infantry battalion
- 2nd Battalion, 3rd Field Artillery Regiment --- Field Artillery

* Cavalry Squadron
- Headquarters and headquarters troop
- 3x cavalry troop (3x 12 M3A3 Bradley)
- Tank company (1x 14 M1A2 Abrams)

* Armor Battalion
- Headquarters and headquarters company
- 2x Tank company (2x 14 M1A2 Abrams)
- Mechanized infantry company (1x 14 M2 Bradley)

* Mechanized Infantry Battalion
- Headquarters and headquarters company
- Tank company (1x 14 M1A2 Abrams)
- 2x Mechanized infantry company (2x 14 M2 Bradley)

* Field Artillery Battalion
- Headquarters and headquarters company
- 3x 155mm self-propelled howitzer battery (3x 6 M109A6 Paladin)

* Total
- Armored/Infantry fighting vehicles
  - 36 (Cavalry squadron)
  - 2x 14 (Armor battalions)
  - 2x 14 (Mechanized infantry battalion)
  - *Total*: 92 armored fighting vehicles
- Main battle tanks
  - 14 (Cavalry squadron)
  - 2x 14 (Armor battalions)
  - 14 (Mechanized infantry battalion)
  - *Total*: 84 main battle tanks
- Self-propelled howitzers
  - 3x howitzer battery (3x 6 self-propelled howitzers)
  - *Total*: 18 self-propelled howeitzers
