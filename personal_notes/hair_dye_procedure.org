#+TITLE: Hair Dye Procedure

- Bring dish soap up to bathroom
- Take off shirt
- Apply Vaseline to forehead, sideburns area and neck
- Pour color mix into developer solution and close developer bottle
- Break tip off developer bottle
- Shake thoroughly
- Apply a small amount of hair dye, then put the bottle down and work hair dye into hair with both hands
- Repeat until hair dye is gone
- Wait 5 minutes
- Shower
