* Materials
- Wire brushes
- Sanding sponges
- WD-40
- Steel wool
- Spray paint
- Paper towels
- Safety goggles
- Masks

* Procedure
- Wear masks and safety goggles
- Soak rusty areas with WD-40 and let stand for a few minutes
- Scrub with sanding sponges, wire brushes and steel wool
- Wipe clean with paper towels
- Apply new paint
