#+TITLE: Karaoke

- Not sure we need a karaoke machine
- Most karaoke machines are just bluetooth speakers with microphones
- Use a karaoke app on a phone or tablet to actually play the music
- If we're doing karaoke at the temple, we already have a sound system with mics

* Karaoke Websites/Apps
- Singa
- Karafun
- Smule
- Spotify also has a karaoke mode
