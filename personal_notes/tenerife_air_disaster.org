#+TITLE: Tenerife Air Disaster

* Disaster Details
- March 27, 1977
- Los Rodeos Airport (now called Tenerife North)
- 583 fatalities (deadliest aircraft disaster in history)

* Cause
- KLM Fight 4805 attempted to take off while Pan Am 1736 was still on the runway
- Dense fog meant that KLM 4805 couldn't see the other plane
- Took off with the mistaken understanding that clearance to take off had been granted, when, in fact, it had not
- Both planes had Gran Canaria airport as their original destination
- However Gran Canaria was closed due to a bomb attack by the Canary Islands Independence Movement
- Radio interference meant that ATC couldn't communicate to KLM 4805 that it didn't actually have takeoff clearance
- A contributing factor to the accident was the use of nonstandard phrases by the ATC which gave the KLM pilots the mistaken impression that they were cleared for takeoff
