#+TITLE: 2023 Mackinac Island Trip

* Trip Dates
- Saturday 2023-08-12 --- Monday 2023-08-14
- Sunrise at 6:30 am
- Sunset at 8:45 pm

* Ferry From Mackinaw City to Mackinac Island
- Departs roughly every 15 minutes from 8:00am to 10:00pm
- Multiple ferry operators
  - Shepler's Ferry --- has a $90 3-adult package deal 
  - Mackinac Island Ferry Company --- $31/adult if tickets bought online


* Trip Itinerary

** Saturday 2023-08-12
- Be at parents' house by 8:00am
- Visit Mackinac Bridge View Park --- Hours: 7:00am - 9:00pm, take photos of the bridge --- is on the way to Mackinaw City
- Drive to Mackinaw City --- arrive at roughly 5:00pm

** Sunday 2023-08-13
- Old Mackinac Point Lighthouse
  - Hours: 9:00am - 5:30pm
  - Admission: $9.75
  - Tours at 9:30am, 10:30am, 11:00am, 1:00pm, 1:30pm, 2:30pm, 3:00pm, 3:30pm
- Take the ferry across to Mackinac Island
- Walk around the island
  - Arch Rock
  - Soldier's Garden
  - Fort Mackinac
  - Butterfly House
- Eat lunch at the Grand Hotel
  - Grand Hotel has 13 restaurants
  - Potential ones that would be a good fit
    - The Gate House
    - Mackinac Island Pizza Company
- Visit Sault Ste. Marie (about 1 hr north of Mackinaw City)
  - Soo Locks
  - Museum Ship Valley Camp

** Monday 2023-08-14
- Drive home
