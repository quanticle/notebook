#+TITLE: Hiccup Cure

Source: https://www.theatlantic.com/ideas/archive/2023/02/scientific-hiccup-cures-hiccaway-straw-ssmi-breathing/673151/

- Exhale completely
- Inhale a deep breath
- Wait 10 seconds
- Without exhaling, inhale a little bit more
- Wait 5 seconds
- Without exhaling, inhale a bit more again
- Exhale
