* Star Wars Visions
- Episode 1: Kamikaze Douga - Kurosawa homage, very nice samurai duel, told in the style of Star Wars
- Episode 2: Studio Coloredo - Good art, but the story fell a little flat with me
- Episode 3: Studio Trigger - Something both magnificent and ridiculous at the same time, as usual; it's like Kill La Kill with lightsabers, what more do you want?
- Episode 4: Kinema Citrus - Amazing art, understated story, even better than Trigger's entry - similar to Kamikaze Douga's entry, but with a more uplifting note at the end
- Episode 5: Production IG - Neat story, but it doesn't really fit with the broader Star Wars plotline
- Episode 6: Science Saru - Nice watercolor art style
- Episode 7: Studio Trigger (again) - I liked this more than Trigger's other episode; this one had a nice lightsaber fight
- Episode 8: Geno Studio - This one's for feep
- Episode 9: Science Saru (again) - Had a completely different art style and tone from the previous entry
