#+TITLE: Garage Door Sensor Replacement

* Tools and Materials
- New sensors and wire
- Wire snips/stripper
- Electrical tape or plastic connectors

* Procedure
- Turn the garage door opener off
- For each sensor
  - Snip the wires going to the sensors
  - Remove sensor from bracket (or remove bracket entirely if necessary)
  - Separate the wire pair
  - Strip the insulation such that there's approximately 1/2" of bare wire showing
  - Split the new sensor wire
  - Strip the new sensor wire such that there's approximately 1/2" of bare wire showing
  - Connect black to black and white to white with wire nuts
- Test new sensor by turning on garage door opener and checking that the green light is steady with no flickering
