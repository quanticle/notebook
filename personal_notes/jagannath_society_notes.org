* Jagganath Society

** Annual Presidency Transfer
- Google account
- File an amendment with the Minnesota Secretary of State Office to update the current officeholders
  - Log in to the [[https://mblsportal.sos.state.mn.us/Business/Search][Business Filings Online]] site
  - Search for your business
  - Click on "Details"
  - Click on "File Amendment/Renewal"
  - Choose your amendment or renewal from the list of amendments and renewals available
    - Some filings may require you to fill out and upload a PDF form
  - Complete the required information and/or upload the PDF form
  - Review the filing, then click submit
  - Click "Pay without Registering" on the payment form
  - After you've completed the payment, you will be sent to a page with a payment confirmation number -- keep the confirmation number in case it's needed for further questions
  - After the filing has been processed, you will receive an e-mail with a link to a copy of the filing
