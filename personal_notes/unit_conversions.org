* Weight/Mass
- 1 ounce = 28.34 g
- 1 pound = 0.453 kg
- 1 ton = 0.907 metric tons

* Volume
- 1 cu. in. = 16.387 cubic cm
- 1 fl. oz. = 29.57 mL
- 1 cup = 236.592 mL
- 1 pint = 0.473 L
- 1 quart = 0.946 L
- 1 gallon = 3.785 L

* Area
- 1 acre = 4046.87 sq. m
- 1 acre = 0.404687 hectare
- 1 square inch = 6.4516 sq. cm
- 1 square foot = 0.09290 sq. m

* Length
- 1 inch = 2.54 cm
- 1 foot = 30.48 cm
- 1 yard = 0.91 m
- 1 mile = 1.61 km

* Temperature
- Fahrenheit to Celsius: \(c = \frac{5}{9}\left(f - 32\right)\)
- Celsius to Fahrenheit: \(f = \frac{9}{5}c + 32\)

* Time
- 60 seconds = 1 minute
- 3600 seconds = 1 hour
- 1440 minutes = 1 day
- 86400 seconds = 1 day
- 168 hours = 1 week
