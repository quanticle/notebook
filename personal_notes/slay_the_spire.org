#+TITLE: Slay The Spire

* Ironclad

** Strength Build
- Strength builders
  - Spot Weakness
  - Flex
    - Grants two strength at the start of the turn
    - Strength goes down by two at the end of the turn
    - Strength loss can be mitigated with Artifact
  - Inflame - priority for upgrade
  - Demon Form
  - Limit Break - upgrade to get rid of the Exhaust effect
- Attack cards
  - Whirlwind
  - Sword Boomerang
  - Twin Btrike
  - Heavy Blade - especially good when upgraded
  - Reaper - important source of healing, scales with strength
- Relics
  - Sling - start elite fights with 2 strength
  - Girya - gain strength at rest sites
  - Red skull - grants 3 extra strength when below 50% health
  - Mutagenic strength
    - Grants 3 strength at the start of combat
    - Strength is lost at the end of the first turn
    - However this loss can be prevented via Artifact
  - Shruiken - playing 3 attacks in one turn increases strength
  - Self-forming clay - taking damage grants block next turn
  - Horn cleat - grants 14 block on the second turn

** Rampage Build
- 1 Rampage that you keep cycling to increase its damage
- Keep the deck /small/
- Remove cards at every opportunity
- Add cards that have cycling/card draw
  - Warcry
  - Headbutt
  - Battle Trance
- Add cards that make the enemy Vulnerable
  - Bash - especially good when upgraded
  - Uppercut - applies Vulnerable and Weak
  - Thunderclap - AOE Vulnerable
- Double Tap is an optional extra that will allow you to hit twice with Rampage
- Relics
  - Bag of Marbles - applies 1 Vulnerable to all enemies at the start of combat
  - Paper Phrog - enemies take 75% extra damage when Vulnerable, rather than 50% extra
  - Peace Pipe - allows you to remove cards at rest sites 
- Strategy
  - Get Rampage in your hand
  - Make enemy vulnerable
  - Keep the deck small
  - Cycle, cycle, cycle

** Searing Blow Build
- Searing Blow is a card that starts out doing 12 damage, but which can be upgraded infinitely
- The damage formula for Searing Blow is \(\frac{n^2 + 7n}{2} + 12\)
- If you can upgrade this card 10 times, it does more than 100 damage
- What other cards do you need
  - Other than Searing Blow, this deck is pretty similar to the Rampage build
  - Cards that apply Vulnerable (see previous section)
  - Card draw (see previous section)
    - Headbutt
    - Dual Wield
    - Secret Weapon
  - Double Tap - allows you to hit with Searing Blow twice
  - Armaments upgrades the card during combat
- Relics
  - Anything that gets Searing Blow into your hand or helps you get to more rest sites
  - Warped Tongs - upgrades random cards in your hand
  - Necronomicon - Similar to Double Tap
  - Winged Boots - Allows you to jump to rest sites by jumping between paths
  - Paper Phrog - Similar to Rampage build, increases the effect of Vulnerable
